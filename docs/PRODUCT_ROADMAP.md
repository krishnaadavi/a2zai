# Product Roadmap

## Roadmap Principles

- Prioritize product clarity before channel expansion.
- Build retention loops first (follow -> alert -> return).
- Keep AI Education as a secondary but valuable funnel.

## 2026 Q1-Q2 Plan

### Phase 1: Reposition + Intelligence Foundation (Now)

Goals:
- Reframe A2Z AI from "AI learning/news portal" to "AI intelligence platform."
- Ship watchlist-ready surfaces in web IA.

Deliverables:
- New homepage information architecture centered on signals.
- Updated global navigation and primary CTAs.
- Intelligence-focused section scaffolding: signal feed, watchlist updates, funding radar.
- Preserve and de-emphasize Learn section.

### Phase 2: Watchlists + Alerts MVP

Goals:
- Enable personalized tracking behavior.

Deliverables:
- Follow/unfollow for companies, startups, and models.
- Watchlist pages and event timeline.
- Alert preferences (email and in-app).
- Daily brief personalized to followed entities.

### Phase 3: Mobile Alignment + iOS Launch

Goals:
- Launch iOS with intelligence-first onboarding and watchlist utility.

Deliverables:
- Mobile IA aligns with web intelligence pillars.
- iOS auth and release hardening complete.
- TestFlight rollout then App Store submission.

### Phase 4: Monetization and Premium Intelligence

Goals:
- Introduce revenue layer with clear value exchange.

Deliverables:
- Free vs Pro limits (watchlist count, alert depth, brief detail).
- Premium weekly investor brief.
- Optional team plan with shared watchlists.

## KPI Framework

- Activation: users who follow 5+ entities in first 3 days.
- Retention: D1, D7, W4 returning users.
- Engagement: alert open rate and watchlist revisit rate.
- Conversion: free to paid conversion and trial completion.
- Quality: % feed items with structured signal metadata.
